About Us


Welcome to Bookit, a havens for book lovers with comfy reading of a nooks curated
selections of new and used books. Bookit was founded in 2019, and it is located in
Ogbomosho Oyo, State. They Specialize in all genres of books, and sells at reasonable
price throughout Nigeria.
